Resume - Suzana Evans

Summary

I started my career in the Australian Public Service where I predominantly worked in L&D roles. I have held an Assistant Director role at both IP Australia and APVMA (Australian Pesticides and Veterinary Medicines Authority). I am currently the Learning and Development (L&D) Manager of MPS. Within my vetting career, I have also worked as a Vetting Analyst (PV) and Quality Assurance officer (PV). I also previously managed and delivered the Certificate IV in Personnel Security within my role as MPS RTO Manager.
